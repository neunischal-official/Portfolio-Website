About Me

I'm an Electrical Engineer

I’m a passionate electrical engineer focused on turning innovative ideas into practical, tech-driven solutions for a smarter future. With a deep interest in hardware, web development, poster design, IoT, and simulations, I thrive at the intersection of creativity and technology.

I'm proficient in tools like Arduino, MATLAB, Python, and AutoCAD—leveraging them to bring my concepts to life through experimentation and smart design. While I’m still building my portfolio of real-world projects, my curiosity and hands-on approach drive me to keep learning and improving every day.

My ultimate goal is to contribute meaningfully to the field of electrical engineering in ways that improve lives and communities. I firmly believe that engineers are the ones who transform dreams into reality—and I’m committed to being one of them.
